HOW TO PLAY SOLITAIRE DUEL

Welcome to SOLITAIRE! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Move all cards to the 4 foundations (top-right) in ascending suit order (A-K).
  • Tableau columns: Build columns down in alternating colors (e.g. Red Queen on Black King).
  • Stock pile: Click the top-left draw pile to flip cards over to the waste pile.
  • Real-time Race: Duel your opponent live. Every card placed scores points. Most points wins!
