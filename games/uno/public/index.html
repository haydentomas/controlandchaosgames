HOW TO PLAY UNO DUEL

Welcome to UNO! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Be the first player to shed all cards in your hand.
  • Matching: Match the top card of the discard pile by color, number, or action symbol.
  • Special Cards: Use Skip, Reverse, Draw 2, and Wild cards to disrupt the opponent.
  • Draw Penalty: If you cannot play a card, you must draw a card from the deck.
  • Shouting: Press the UNO button when down to your final card!
