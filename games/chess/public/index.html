HOW TO PLAY CHESS

Welcome to Chess Duel! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Capture the opponent's King to win the match.
  • Movement: Standard chess movement applies. Select a piece to highlight legal destination squares, then click a target square to move.
  • Piece Capture: Land on a square occupied by an opponent's piece to capture it and remove it from the board.
  • Winning: The game ends immediately when a King is captured. Press RESET MATCH to start a new duel.
