HOW TO PLAY CONNECT FOUR

Welcome to Connect Four! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Be the first player to align four of your colored tokens (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  • Gameplay: On your turn, choose a column by hovering and clicking. The token falls to the lowest available space in that column.
  • Alternating Turns: Players take turns one after the other. Keep an eye on your opponent's tokens to block them!
  • Winning: The match ends immediately when a line of four is formed. Press RESET MATCH to play again!
