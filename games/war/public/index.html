HOW TO PLAY WAR ARCADE BATTLE

Welcome to WAR! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Win battle rounds to score the most points before your deck runs dry.
  • Battle: Select 1 card from your 5 hand cards to play face down. Both cards flip together.
  • Winner: Higher rank wins the round and scores points. Suits resolve rank ties.
  • Power-ups: Use SHIELD to block a loss, or DOUBLE to double your points on a win!
