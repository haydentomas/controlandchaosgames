HOW TO PLAY MEMORY MATCH

Welcome to Memory Match! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Find and match pairs of identical cards to score points.
  • Gameplay: Take turns flipping two cards face-up. If the cards match, they are removed and you score 1 point. If they do not match, they flip back over.
  • Alternating Turns: If you find a match, you keep your turn! If not, the turn passes to your opponent.
  • Winning: The player with the most matches when all cards are cleared wins the game.
