HOW TO PLAY TIC TAC TOE

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Align three of your marks (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) on the grid.
  • Gameplay: Take turns placing your symbol (X or O) on an empty square of the 3x3 grid.
  • Winning: The first player to align three symbols wins the round. If all spaces are filled without alignment, it's a draw.
