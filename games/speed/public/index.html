HOW TO PLAY SPEED

Welcome to SPEED! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Be the first to shed all cards from your hand and reserve decks.
  • Gameplay: Click a card from your 4 active slots, then click either center pile to play it.
  • Valid Play: Card must be exactly 1 rank higher or lower than the top center card (Aces loop with K and 2).
  • Stuck: If neither player can move, click STUCK to flip new cards from the reserve.
