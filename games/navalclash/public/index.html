HOW TO PLAY NAVAL CLASH

Welcome to Naval Clash! Here is how to play:

  • Objective: Sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours.
  • Grid Setup: Position your fleet of 5 ships onto your grid. Click cells to place ships, or use AUTO-PLACE to scramble positions.
  • Combat: Take turns firing shots at the enemy's radar grid. A hit is marked in red, a miss in white. Landing a hit grants you an extra turn!
  • Winning: The duel ends when one player's entire fleet is sunk.
